Welcome to TechMntor.

TechMntor is an initiative to empower the women in technology by connecting them to mentor who can help in the form of role modeling, support, and coaching.

We Inspire

Every day we present a story of a woman just like you who has done wonders in field of technology.

Looking for a mentor?

Check out our mentor-Mentee section, you will definitely find an expert to mentor you.
